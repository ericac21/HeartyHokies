Dessert

Craving something sweet and tasty? Try some of these recipes.


Mug Cake

Satisfies the chocolate craving 100%

💡 Instant mug cake packets (in different flavors as well): Readily available from stores like Kroger, Walmart, and Target

💡 Do it yourself mug cake: Gather the right ingredients and heat it up

📌 Check out this site for a delicious chocolatey mug cake recipe!




Yogurt-Covered Blueberries

The right balance of fruity, icy, and creamy all together

💡 Frozen yogurt-covered blueberries: Only works if your fridge has a freezer component, if not...utilize your friends :)

📌 Check out this site for a great recipe for yogurt-covered blueberries!




Cinnamon Apples

A warm and wonderfully spiced treat

💡 Microwave cinnamon apples: Cut apples, toss in spice + sugar, and microwave

📌 Check out this site for a way to make cinnamon apples in a Ziploc bag!




Cookie Dough

A perfect soft, sweet, chewy dessert

💡 Ingredients are easy to find and can be bought in local grocery stores (Don't forget the chocolate chips!)

📌 Follow this recipe for the perfect edible cookie dough!!




Hot Chocolate

The quickest and most satisfying of these sweets

💡 Instant hot chocolate: You can find this in almost any store. Don't forget to buy milk, or use water!

💡 Heat up your milk/water in the microwave and put in the hot cocoa mix! Then, enjoy~

Chocolate-Covered Strawberries

Who doesn't love strawberries and chocolate?

💡 Ingredients: Chocolate, strawberries, and sometimes coconut oil

📌 Check out this site for the easy chocolate-covered strawberry recipe!
